Sources

For creating an informative map with US county boundaries, one reliable and widely-used source is the "US County Boundaries" dataset available from the United States Census Bureau. The Census Bureau provides authoritative and up-to-date geographic data, including shapefiles and geospatial data, that delineate the boundaries of each county in the United States.
